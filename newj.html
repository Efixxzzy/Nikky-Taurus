ADENIKE IBIROGBA

Managing Director/CEO

Mrs Adenike Ibirogba is the Managing Director of Nikky Taurus Nigeria Limited. She graduated with a bachelor’s degree in Law and Politics from the University of Keele, United Kingdom in 1984 and has a master’s degree also in law from the University of London. She was called to the Nigerian Bar in 1986.
In 1995, she founded Nikky Taurus Nigeria Limited, a transportation and leasing company.  Tasked with providing excellence through the leasing of vehicles, her passion, inter-personal skills and communication proficiency has led her to repeatedly prove her ability to deliver strong and sustained results. She has indeed met and exceeded corporate goals and challenges in the world of transportation. She is a board member of Special Olympics Nigeria and a member of Chartered Institute of Transport & Logistics United Kingdom (MILT), Institute of Chartered Mediators & Conciliators (MICMC) and an almuna of Lagos Business School.

AYOWANDE VIVIAN AYO

Executive Director

In Automania, She successfully managed the internationally recognized and prestigious annual Lagos Motorfair from 1989 – 1996.
