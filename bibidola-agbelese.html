BIBIDOLA AGBELESE

HR Manager

Bibidola Agbelese  has a Bachelors (BSc.) and Master of Science (MSc.) in Geography and Planning both from the University of Lagos.In 2009 he joined  B.A Adigun and Associates, a firm of Chartered Financial Consultants, as a Trainee Human Resources Officer. In 2012, he moved on to Zeta-Web Nigeria Limited where, through hard and diligence, he became the Head Of Human Resources. Bibidola then joined Nikky Taurus in January 2017 as The Human Resources Manager.
Bibidola’s wealth of experience lies in Compensation and Benefits, Recruitment, Labor Relations, Performance Management as well as HR Analytics.  A member of the Society for Human Resource Managers (SHRM), a certified Human Resource Business Partner (HRBP) and also a student member of the Chartered Institute of Personnel Managers Nigeria (CIPM), Bibidola’s passion for solving people’s problems is his driving force in the HR industry.

We know the difference is in the details and that’s why our car rental services, in the tourism and business industry, stand out for their quality.
