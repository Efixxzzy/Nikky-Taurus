Welcome to Nikky Taurus.
Nikky Taurus Nigeria Limited is a dynamic organisation registered in Nigeria to provide transportation services through Vehicle Hire & Leasing, Fleet Management, Logistics Consultancy, Support services and Manpower Recruitment Services. Nikky Taurus commenced operations in 1995 and has consistently positioned itself as a strong and reliable brand, specialising in Vehicle Hire & Leasing in Nigeria and within the ECOWAS sub region.
As an integral part of its products and services, Nikky Taurus values customer convenience, reliability and sustainability. The organisation manages a fleet of over 450 vehicles, giving it a broad reach and expertise in the industry, and also positioning it as the number one go-to partner in adding measurable value to customers and stakeholders.
Nikky Taurus targets all its bouquet of service offerings at corporate bodies, embassies, educational institutions, religious institutions and individuals.

Mission & Vision

To be the leading vehicle hire / leasing and other related support services provider in Nigeria by providing safe, reliable and affordable transportation services to clients in Nigeria and within the ECOWAS sub-region.

We will lead our industry by defining service excellence and efficiently serving value-conscious customers for all of their car rental occasions.

Our mission is to provides safe, reliable, and cost-effective transportation services to corporate bodies in Nigeria and within the ECOWAS sub-region, through Vehicle Hire & Leasing, Fleet Management, Logistics Consultancy, Support services and Manpower Recruitment Services.


We will endeavour to earn customers loyalty by delivering more than expected, being fair and doing everything possible to provide unparalleled services thus setting higher standards in car rentals.

Our business is built on the following values which shapes all our actions and our relationship with our employees and clients.
Professionalism: We adhere to very high ethical standards and we deal with our customers with all sense of professionalism.
Hard Work: We believe that strong challenge is commonly connected with strong results and as such encourage hard work.
Loyalty: We understand the importance of reliability and so we go the extra mile to strengthen our relationship with our employees and customers.
Discipline: We pay attention to good conduct while dealing with our customers at all levels.
Integrity: We meet the highest ethical standards in all our business dealings. We do what we say we will do. We are honest with ourselves and with others.
